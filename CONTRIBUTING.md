# Contributing

Contributions are welcome when they preserve the experiment's evidence boundary.

1. Open an issue describing the scientific or engineering question.
2. Add or update tests for behavioral changes.
3. Keep calibration and evaluation ensembles separate.
4. Do not tune thresholds on the reported evaluation seeds.
5. Label illustrative mechanisms as illustrative; do not imply flight validation.
6. Include machine-readable outputs for any new benchmark.

By contributing, you agree that your contribution is licensed under the repository's MIT License.
